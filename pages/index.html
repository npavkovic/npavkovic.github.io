Oracle

As a visual designer at Oracle (2012-2014), I contributed to the print designs of the flagship Oracle magazine as well as Profit magazine (a lifestyle publication) and the hybrid online/print magazine, Java.

Ziff-Davis Verlag

Arriving in Munich in 1994 for a short redesign assignment for Ziff-Davis Verlag, I stayed on through 1997, serving as Art Director and, later, Creative Director. Publications included PC Professionell (analogous to PC Magazine in the U.S.) and the launch of pl@net, an internet culture magazine. My staff included junior art directors, a layout department and a photo studio.

 {{ createSlides("ziff-davis", "Publication Design") }}

Pulp Magazine

Founded in 1988 with photographer/illustrator Francisco Caceres, this DIY magazine entered the scene during the last (?) flowering of print magazines prior to the arrival of the web. In addition to providing me a place to spread my wings as a publication designer and writer, it was a valuable outlet for creatives of all types in Chicago.
